Frequently Asked Questions

How do visas for China work? expand_more
We provide full concierge support for visa applications. Whether you need a Tourist (L) or Business (M) visa, our team handles the documentation, invitation letters, and filing process to ensure a smooth approval.
When is the best time to visit East Asia? expand_more
Spring (March–May) for cherry blossoms and Autumn (September–November) for temperate weather and fall foliage are generally considered the best times for general tourism.
Can you arrange high-level business meetings? expand_more
Yes. Through our Trade arm, we have access to various industry hubs and can arrange factory tours, government meetings, and local chamber of commerce introductions.
How are dietary restrictions handled? expand_more
Your guide will be briefed on any allergies or dietary preferences (Vegan, Halal, Gluten-Free). We pre-vet all restaurants and provide "preference cards" in the local language.
Is travel insurance included? expand_more
Standard travel insurance is not included in the base price, but we offer a comprehensive "Digital Diplomat" coverage add-on that includes medical evacuation and high-value equipment protection.
