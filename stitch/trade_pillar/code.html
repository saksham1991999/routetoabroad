What is the Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

expand_more

MOQs vary by industry. Typically, for custom electronics it starts at 500-1000 units, while for industrial machinery it can be a single unit. We specialize in negotiating MOQs for growing businesses.

How do you ensure Quality Assurance?

expand_more

Our inspectors are on the factory floor during production and before sealing the container. We follow AQL standard (Acceptable Quality Limit) protocols.

What are the typical shipping timeframes?

expand_more

Air freight takes 3-7 days. Sea freight from China ports to Mumbai or Mundra typically takes 18-25 days depending on the service route.

Can you handle Customs and BIS certifications?

expand_more

Yes, we provide full support for BIS, WPC, and other regulatory filings required by the Indian government for specific electronic imports.

What are the payment terms (LC/TT)?

expand_more

Standard terms are 30% deposit via TT and 70% against Bill of Lading. For established enterprise clients, we can facilitate LC (Letter of Credit) arrangements.

Do you support OEM and Private Labeling?

expand_more

Absolutely. We help you find factories that specialize in white-labeling and can manage the branding and packaging customization process for you.
